Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my company password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your company email and follow the instructions sent to you. If you don't receive an email within 5 minutes, raise a ticket.
My VPN is not connecting. What should I do?
First, ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try restarting the VPN client. If the issue persists, check if your VPN certificate has expired and raise a ticket with priority set to High.
How long does it take to resolve a ticket?
Critical tickets are addressed within 1 hour, High within 4 hours, Medium within 1 business day, and Low within 3 business days. Our AI Resolution Rate is currently 72%.
My Outlook email is not syncing. How to fix?
Go to File → Account Settings → Repair your account. If that doesn't work, try removing and re-adding your account. Still stuck? Raise an Email Issue ticket and our team will assist.
How do I request a new software license?
Create a ticket with the category "Software Request", mention the software name and business justification. Approval typically takes 1–2 business days.
How do I track my submitted ticket?
Go to "My Tickets" in the sidebar. You can filter by status: Open, In Progress, Solved, or Not Viewed. Click the eye icon on any ticket to view full details.
