# SMARTHELPDESK AI: INTELLIGENT IT SERVICE TICKETING & SUPPORT AUTOMATION PLATFORM

---

## 1. PROJECT TITLE

**SmartHelpdesk AI: Intelligent IT Service Ticketing & Support Automation Platform**

---

## 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

Large organizations such as POWERGRID use multiple platforms for IT support and issue reporting, including GLPI, Solman, email systems, and other internal tools. Employees often face difficulties in determining which platform to use for a specific issue, leading to delays in ticket creation, duplicated requests, poor user experience, and reduced employee satisfaction.

The current process requires manual ticket categorization, assignment, and monitoring, which increases the workload on IT support teams and slows down issue resolution. Common issues such as password resets, VPN access requests, software installation requests, and account unlocks consume significant support resources despite being repetitive in nature.

Furthermore, the lack of centralized ticket visibility and intelligent analytics makes it difficult for management to identify recurring issues, optimize support operations, and improve service quality.

To address these challenges, an AI-powered centralized ticketing solution is needed that can consolidate tickets from multiple sources, automate classification and routing, provide self-service support, and deliver faster issue resolution through intelligent automation.

---

## 3. PROJECT OBJECTIVES

### Primary Objectives

* Develop a centralized AI-powered ticket management platform.
* Integrate tickets from multiple sources such as email, chatbot, GLPI, Solman, and web portals.
* Automatically classify tickets using Natural Language Processing (NLP).
* Route tickets intelligently to the appropriate IT support team.
* Provide self-service resolution for common IT issues through AI chatbots.
* Reduce ticket response and resolution time.
* Improve employee satisfaction through a unified support experience.

### Secondary Objectives

* Minimize manual intervention in ticket handling.
* Improve resource utilization of IT support teams.
* Generate insights from historical ticket data.
* Enhance organizational productivity through faster issue resolution.
* Support configurable email and SMS notifications.
* Enable knowledge sharing through automated article recommendations.

## 7. EXPECTED OUTCOME

The proposed SmartHelpdesk AI platform will serve as a centralized and intelligent IT support management system capable of handling tickets from multiple sources through a single interface.

By leveraging Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and intelligent automation, the system will significantly reduce manual ticket processing, improve response times, and enhance overall employee satisfaction.

The AI chatbot will provide instant support for common issues, while automated classification and routing will ensure that tickets reach the correct support teams without delays. The knowledge base recommendation engine will promote self-service support and continuous organizational learning.

Overall, the platform will improve operational efficiency, reduce support costs, and provide a seamless IT support experience for employees.

## 8. IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Requirement Analysis

* Study existing ticketing platforms such as GLPI and Solman.
* Identify integration requirements and workflows.
* Define ticket categories and support processes.

### Phase 2: System Development

* Develop centralized ticket management platform.
* Implement chatbot and NLP engine.
* Build ticket classification and routing modules.

### Phase 3: Integration & Testing

* Integrate email, GLPI, Solman, and chatbot channels.
* Perform functional and security testing.
* Validate AI classification accuracy.

### Phase 4: Pilot Deployment

* Deploy in selected departments.
* Collect employee feedback.
* Monitor ticket resolution improvements.

### Phase 5: Full-Scale Deployment

* Roll out across the organization.
* Enable real-time monitoring and analytics.
* Continuously improve AI models using ticket history.

### Phase 6: Monitoring & Optimization

* Track KPIs such as response time and resolution rate.
* Enhance chatbot knowledge base.
* Generate insights for continuous process improvement.
